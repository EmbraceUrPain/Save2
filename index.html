Welcome to the Black Souls Community

Despair. Fear, Madness, and Lust ,once again. An original work based on "Alice in Wonderland" and "Through the Looking-Glass." Dark Fairy Tale Fantasy x Psychological Horror RPG focusing on the themes of "Exploration of a Mad World" and "Life or Death Combat against Demonbeasts".

[This wiki is a work-in-progress and is currently incomplete in many places. The work on creating the new pages continues.]

WELCOME TO WONDERLAND!! In Black Souls, you play as an undead who wakes up to the entire world.. crumbling around him? People turning into monsters by some weird fog covering the world, it’s all so surreal. You, along with many companions along the way, fight to stop this fog while avoiding any threats you may encounter. You finally stop the fog and a familiar, dear blonde-haired young girl greets you. Time passes and you find yourself in a rabbit hole filled with dead knights, with only one thought in mind—to find that blonde haired girl, Alice.

Black Souls is an RPG developed by Eeny, meeny, miny, moe?. Black Souls is inspired by many pieces of literature and many writers, namely but not limited to Lewis Carroll’s "Alice in Wonderland", Hans Christian Andersen’s "Little Match Girl", HP Lovecraft’s "Cthulhu Mythos" and Charles Perrault’s "Little Red Riding Hood".
